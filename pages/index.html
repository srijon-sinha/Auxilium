Getting help

It is extremely important to seek the right kind of help for a specific psychological disorder. The premium get help feature ensures that you are connected to a specialist who will treat you according to your needs and requirements, based on your psychological profile.

Panic attack detection

Panic attacks, when they come out of nowhere, can be extremely scary. Stuck in that moment, unable to breath – it truly is a terrifying idea. Auxilium takes steps to help you through these moments. Using your phone's built in gyroscope, it can detect when your hands are shaking much more than usual (by establishing a baseline over time using trained neural networks) and will send you a notification. Sally while guide you through the attack and help you calm down.

Psychological diagnosis

Auxilium builds upon the work of psychologists across the years, utilising elements from reputed tests in order to form a reliable diagnosis with an extremely low margin of error. The first step is a basic diagnostic test, following which you can take more specific tests for the psychological disorders you may have. Your psychological profile is constantly evolving, and you can keep updating your diagnoses through your interactions with Sally.

Daily checkups

Working on your mental health is a constant process, and you are bound to go through lows and highs. The daily checkup feature in Auxilium gives you a means to introspect and tackle the feelings you may have been repressing. In the long run, this can make all the difference towards improving your overall mental state.
